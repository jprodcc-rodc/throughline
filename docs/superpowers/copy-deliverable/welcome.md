# Welcome to Rodix

You signed up. Here's what the next five minutes look like.

You'll land on the chat. Type something — not a test query, an actual thing you've been turning over. A decision, a worry, a question you keep coming back to. Talk like you would to ChatGPT. While you talk, Rodix is doing one extra thing in the background: pulling out the structure of what you said into a card. Topic, concern, hope, open question. Four fields, your own wording. The card shows up in your Vault, and you can read it, edit it, delete it, or leave it alone. That's the loop. Do it a few times across a few days, and the cards start adding up into something useful — a record of how your thinking evolved, in your own words, that you can take with you.

## What Rodix is for

Rodix is a memory layer for AI chat, built for thinking, not for engagement. The bet is that if you spend hours a week working through hard things with AI — career calls, projects, relationships, anything that doesn't get solved in one prompt — the value isn't in any single answer. The value is in the spine of conversations: what you concluded, what you left open, what you keep coming back to. Most AI tools throw that away. Rodix saves it as cards you own and brings them back when they're relevant. Same memory works whether you're using GPT-5, Claude, Gemini, or whatever wins next year. Export is one click, markdown files, your hard drive.

## What Rodix is not

If you ask AI "what's a good restaurant in Lisbon" twice a week and that's it, you don't need this. ChatGPT's fine. Rodix is also not a journaling app — you don't write into it; it writes itself from your conversations. Not a knowledge base for already-organized notes. Not a productivity tool. Not therapy — if you're working through a mental health crisis, Rodix is not built for that, and the FAQ has resources. The user we're building for is someone who's already three AI-tools deep, has tried "memory" features and quietly turned them off, and wants the spine of their thinking to survive between sessions. If that's you, just open the chat and start.
