## 2. What Rodix is

Rodix is an AI memory layer. You have a conversation with an AI; Rodix extracts structured cards from what you said (typically four fields: topic, concern, hope, question); when you bring up a related subject later, Rodix surfaces the relevant past cards so the conversation can continue from where you left off.

Rodix is a thinking partner. It is not a therapist, a doctor, a lawyer, an accountant, a financial advisor, or any other regulated professional. The AI's replies are not professional advice. We will say more about this in Section 8.

The AI inference itself is performed by a third-party model provider (currently Anthropic, accessed via OpenRouter). Rodix is the layer on top — the memory, the cards, the recall, the export. We may change the underlying model provider; the cards and your data remain yours.

You need an account to use Rodix. The account is tied to your email address. You agree:

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Account. Deletion removes your conversations and cards within 30 days from our primary database, and within 90 days from backups. See the Privacy Policy for details.

**The service is provided "as is" and "as available."** We do not warrant that the service will be uninterrupted, error-free, or fit for any specific purpose.

**AI replies are not professional advice.** The AI in Rodix produces text that may resemble advice. It is not advice from a doctor, therapist, lawyer, accountant, financial advisor, or any other licensed professional. Do not rely on it for medical, legal, mental-health, financial, or similar decisions. AI is a thinking partner, not a substitute for professional services. ## 14. Conversation persistence

Rodix is built around persistent memory of your conversations. The product purpose is for past thinking to remain available for future use; deletion is therefore a deliberate user action, not an automatic background process.

While your account is active, your conversations and cards are stored in your vault until you delete them. Backups exist for service reliability per the Privacy Policy. The export feature (Settings → Export) gives you a markdown copy of your vault that you can take with you at any time.
