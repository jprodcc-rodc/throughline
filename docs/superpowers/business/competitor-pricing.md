## 1. Direct comparables — AI memory / thinking-partner / second-brain

### Mem.ai (`get.mem.ai/pricing`, support article archived)

- **Free tier** — basic features, individual use
- **Mem X / Mem Pro:** $10-12 / month (Mem Pro at $12/mo unlimited tier, no individual annual discount per support article)
- **Team:** $15 / month / user
- **Enterprise:** custom
- **Recent changes:** Mem Pro unlimited tier launched at $12/mo with no annual discount on individual plan — a deliberate flat-pricing move
- **User-complaint signals:** Mem.ai has visible distribution-failure shape (per `position-strategy.md` §8: *"Mem.ai's failure was distribution-shaped, not product-shaped"*). Public discussion thinning over 2024-2025.

### Reflect.app (`reflect.app`)

- **Single plan** — $10 / month (annual billing) — simple flat pricing
- **14-day free trial**
- AI features (GPT-4 + Whisper) bundled
- End-to-end encryption included
- **Recent changes:** Has held $10 flat-annual since 2023 — pricing stability is part of brand
- **User-complaint signals:** Smaller user base; positive reviews skew toward power users / journalers

### Tana.inc (`outliner.tana.inc/pricing`, updated 2025-10-13)

- **Free:** 500 AI credits/month, 100 min meetings, limited transcription
- **Plus:** standard pricing (range ~$10-12/mo per third-party reviews) saves 22% annually; **2,000 AI credits/month**
- **Pro** (formerly "Core"): saves 22% annually; **5,000 AI credits/month**
- **Student/NGO:** Plus $5/mo; Pro $9/mo annual
- **Recent changes:** "Core" rebranded to "Pro" October 2025; credit allocations restructured
- **User-complaint signals:** AI-credit metering creates anxiety; users complain about consumption opacity

### Heptabase (`heptabase.com/pricing`)

- **Pro:** $8.99 / month annual ($11.99 monthly) — unlimited notes, 100 AI credits/month
- **Premium:** $17.99 / month annual — adds unlimited PDF parsing, AI Tutor, 1,800 AI credits
- **Premium+:** $53.99 / month annual — 8,100 AI credits
- **Free trial:** 7 days; no permanent free tier
- **Annual discount:** 25% (save 3 months on annual)
- **No student discount**
- **Recent changes:** Tiered AI-credit segmentation introduced — Pro / Premium / Premium+ split is explicit price discrimination on AI usage
- **User-complaint signals:** Heavy users hit AI-credit walls fast on Pro; Premium feels expensive at $18 for solo

### Granola.ai (`granola.ai/pricing`)

- **Basic (Free):** AI meeting notes, limited history, AI chat
- **Business:** $14 / month / user — unlimited notes, advanced AI thinking, integrations
- **Enterprise:** $35 / month / user
- **No annual discount** mentioned on pricing page
- **Recent changes:** Repositioned 2025 from solo to team (meeting-notes context); $14 reflects per-seat team pricing not solo
- **User-complaint signals:** Users on Twitter ask why no solo / annual tier; meeting-notes-only narrows ICP

### Capacities.io (`capacities.io/pricing`)

- **Free:** core product permanent free per company commitment
- **Pro:** $9.99 / month annual ($11.99 monthly) — AI, smart queries, calendar
- **Believer:** $12.49 / month annual ($14.99 monthly) — supports indie development
- **Recent changes:** Pricing held stable through 2024-2026; "Believer" tier is patron-style not feature-different
- **User-complaint signals:** Believer-as-patronage works for the indie-friendly cohort; no major churn signals

### Saner.ai (`saner.ai/pricing`)

- **Free:** 30 AI requests/month, 100 notes, 100MB storage
- **Starter:** $8 / month annual ($12 monthly) — 50 AI requests/day, 1,000 notes, 5GB
- **Standard:** $16 / month annual ($20 monthly) — unlimited AI, unlimited notes, 100GB
- **Recent changes:** Coupon discounts visible (25% / $60 off) suggesting weak conversion
- **User-complaint signals:** ADHD-positioned product with mixed reviews on AI quality

### Limitless / Rewind.ai (`limitless.ai/pricing`, post-acquisition Dec 2025)

- **Free:** 1,200 minutes (20 hours) transcription/month, AI features unlimited
- **Pro:** $19 / month — 100 hours/month, includes Rewind Pro integration
- **Unlimited Monthly:** $49 / month — Pendant required ($99 one-time)
- **Recent changes (major):** **December 2025 — Meta acquired Limitless. Rewind Mac app shut down, screen/audio capture disabled, all remaining users moved to free under Meta's terms.**
- **User-complaint signals:** Massive trust collapse on the Mac product post-acquisition. Privacy-sensitive users churned hard. Cautionary tale for Rodix narrative — *"this is exactly what happens when memory is locked to vendor business model."*

## 2. AI chat baselines — what users compare Rodix against

### ChatGPT Plus (`chat.openai.com`, OpenAI)

- **Free:** limited GPT-5.2 access, daily caps
- **Plus:** $20 / month — current frontier reasoning models, modest caps
- **Pro:** $100 / month (added April 2026 in response to Claude Max) — middle rung
- **Pro Original / Premium Pro:** $200 / month — unlimited frontier reasoning
- **Recent changes:** April 2026 — OpenAI added $100 mid-tier reflecting Claude Max competition pressure
- **Memory feature:** included free, no surcharge — but is the flagship failure mode Rodix critiques

### Claude Pro (`anthropic.com`)

- **Free:** daily caps, mid-tier model
- **Pro:** $20 / month monthly, **$17 / month annual ($200 upfront)** — 14% annual discount
- **Max 5×:** $100 / month — 5× usage caps
- **Max 20×:** $200 / month — 20× usage caps
- **Projects feature:** included — Rodix's Bet 2 critique target
- **Recent changes:** Stable $20 baseline; Max tiers pull engineering / power users upmarket

### Notion AI Add-on (`notion.com/pricing`)

- **Free:** $0 — basic Notion, no AI
- **Plus:** $10 / month annual ($12 monthly)
- **Business:** $15-18 / month annual — **only tier with AI bundled** post-2026 restructure
- **Enterprise:** custom
- **Recent changes (major):** **2026 — Notion AI add-on (formerly $8/member/month standalone) eliminated; AI now exclusive to Business tier** ($18/mo). Free + Plus users cannot access AI features at all. Notable user-complaint trigger.
- **User-complaint signals:** Visible backlash on Twitter / Reddit about AI being paywalled to Business; many users downgraded or migrated

## 3. Cluster summary

**The $20/mo cluster (frontier AI):**
- ChatGPT Plus $20
- Claude Pro $20 ($17 annual)
- Limitless Pro $19

**The $14-18/mo cluster (AI-power-user tools):**
- Granola Business $14
- Heptabase Premium $17.99
- Notion Business $18
- Saner Standard $16

**The $8-12/mo cluster (solo / indie tools):**
- Heptabase Pro $8.99
- Saner Starter $8
- Capacities Pro $9.99
- Reflect $10
- Mem Pro $10-12
- Capacities Believer $12.49

**The "free + power-user-paid" pattern:** Tana, Capacities, Saner, Granola, Notion all ship a permanent free tier with paid upgrade. Mem and Reflect ship trial-only. Heptabase ships 7-day-trial-only. Pattern: most direct second-brain competitors run free-tier-with-AI-credit-meter. Frontier AI baselines run trial-only at $20.

**Annual discount norms:**
- Standard: 17-25% (Heptabase 25%, Tana 22%, Reflect ~17%)
- Outliers: Mem Pro NO annual discount on individual; Granola NO annual mentioned; Claude 14% (modest)
- Recommendation for Rodix: 17-20% range fits norm; deeper discount looks desperate

**Recent-changes signal patterns (Q4 2025 - Q1 2026):**
1. **Limitless / Rewind acquisition** — privacy-product trust collapsed when acquirer's terms applied (Rodix opportunity narrative)
2. **Notion AI paywalled to Business** — visible user backlash; opportunity for Rodix to position as "AI-bundled at solo price"
3. **OpenAI added $100 Pro tier** — premium-tier expansion in frontier AI; doesn't directly compress sub-$20 indie tier
4. **Tana "Core" → "Pro" rebrand** — credit-allocation restructure suggesting margin pressure on AI-meter tools
5. **Mem Pro no-annual-discount** — flat-pricing experiment

## 4. Positioning implications for Rodix

**Where Rodix fits in the cluster.** The friends-intro positions Rodix against ChatGPT Plus / Claude Pro on the AI-axis (memory works on top of those) and against Mem / Reflect / Tana on the second-brain axis. Pricing should signal:

- **Not a frontier AI** — don't price at $20 (would imply competing on chat quality, which Rodix explicitly disclaims: *"Not 'better AI'. The AI underneath is whatever you choose."*)
- **Not a luxury power-user tool** — Heptabase Premium $18 lands wrong for Rodix's Everyman color (anti-target: *"this isn't a luxury thinking tool"*)
- **Aligned with the indie-tool $8-12 band** — Reflect $10, Capacities Pro $10, Heptabase Pro $9, Mem Pro $10-12 are the natural neighborhood

**Anti-patterns to avoid:**
- AI-credit metering — every credit-metered product (Tana, Heptabase, Saner) generates the same complaint shape: opacity. Rodix's brand demands transparency; AI-credit meters are the inverse.
- Team / Enterprise tier — skip for Phase 1 (single-user product per `S10`)
- Patronage / "Believer" tier — Capacities does it well; Rodix could but it's premature in alpha

**Patterns to adopt:**
- Permanent free tier (Tana / Capacities / Saner pattern) OR free-trial-only (Heptabase / Mem / Reflect pattern) — Rodix decision required (Type-A escalation in `pricing-strategy.md`)
- Annual discount 17-20% (Tana 22%, Heptabase 25%, Reflect ~17% — middle of cluster norm)
- Founder pricing for first 100 users (Capacities Believer-like, but time-locked) — Phase 1 alpha specific
