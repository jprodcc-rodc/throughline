## Voice baseline (one register, five calibrations)

The friends-intro register is the master. All five channels speak in the same voice; each calibration shifts cadence and structure, never adjective-stack or hype level.

| Channel | Calibration delta from friends-intro |
|---|---|
| Founder essay | Friends-intro register, expanded. Three-asterisk separators replace "Part 1 / 2 / 3" headings. All parentheticals, em-dashes, dates preserved. |
| HN Show post | Mechanism-first. Cut 30% of warmth, retain 100% of honesty. HN-friendly section breaks ("What it is / How it works / What's missing"). Code-path references allowed. |
| PH launch | Slightly warmer than HN, slightly more visual. First-comment is friends-intro register full-strength; maker comments are shorter, conversational. |
| Twitter thread | Hammered fragments — tweets ARE friends-intro paragraphs forced to ≤280 chars. Em-dash and parenthetical preserved when they fit. Each tweet is a complete claim. |
| Video script | Read-aloud cadence at ~150 wpm. Pauses on dates and on "I killed the project that night." Anonymous = voice-only. |

## What's locked across all five

**The 8-word pitch is verbatim, every channel.** "ChatGPT remembers your name. Rodix remembers your thinking." Hero of essay, closing of thread, on-screen text in video, opening graf of HN post-script, PH title-line.

**The four bets framed as architectural commitments, not features.** Per founder-narrative-arc v1.1 Part 2 P2 fix: Rodix is "all four together"; the moat is structural-incompatibility with frontier-vendor business models, not feature-list differentiation. No channel is allowed to drift into feature-list framing.

**The Gemini "corporate secretary" specific failure mode appears in every channel except video.** Highest-leverage signature wound across all surfaces. Video swaps it for the Sept 3 / Sept 19 / Oct 4 cards on screen — visual content carries the structural argument without narration.

**Volunteered-limit triplet is non-negotiable.** "Server-side recall (so we can't promise zero-knowledge — that would be a lie given the architecture). Encryption hardening on the post-launch roadmap. The actual ownership story is markdown export." Appears verbatim or near-verbatim in essay + HN + PH first-comment. Compressed reference in thread + video.

**Status section at close.** "Solo, anonymous, working out of Asia, second half of a multi-year build. Public launch: weeks, not months." Verbatim wherever it appears. Four facts, no mythology.

**Anti-target sentence is brand-defining.** "If you ask AI 'what's a good restaurant in Lisbon' twice a week and that's it, you don't need this. ChatGPT's fine." Appears in essay closing + thread tweet 8 + PH first-comment. Sends unfit users away.

## Saturated anchors to avoid

- McLuhan "we shape our tools" — Granola + Notion both anchor on this, saturated.
- "Tools for thought" — claimed by Notion lineage; Rodix is not in that lineage by founder positioning (Rodc is anonymous, indie, no Engelbart name-drop).
- "Bicycle for the mind" — Apple-coded.
- "Build in public" / "indie hacker" — LinkedIn founder-coach drift; friends-intro says "anonymous" instead.

---

## Voice tells that distinguish Rodix from neighbors

- vs Anthropic: Rodix is *individual* scale ("I'm a heavy AI user"); Anthropic is institutional ("we believe").
- vs Granola: Rodix refuses pedagogy; no analogies, no "let me explain why this matters."
- vs Linear: Rodix doesn't open with childhood paradox or philosophical wonder; opens with hours-on-keyboard.
- vs Stripe: closer match — heavy-user-fixing-own-problem. But Rodix's anonymity + parenthetical-as-honesty register is its own.
- vs LinkedIn founder-coach: every channel must refuse "passion / journey / mission / scrappy / on a mission to / I'm so excited to share."
