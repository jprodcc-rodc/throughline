## Etymology and meaning

Coined compound: **Rod** (founder pseudonym) + **keep** (EN word: to hold, to retain, to store; also archaic noun "keep" = stronghold tower of a castle, the most defensible space). Carries sovereignty/possession metaphor — the brand-book §4 defining quality.

**Etymology of keep:** Old English *cēpan* (to seize, observe), Middle English shifted to "to retain". The "keep" of a castle is the defensible stronghold — apt for sovereignty/anti-vendor-lock-in messaging.

## Strategic positioning

Rodkeep positions as **the brand that keeps your thinking yours, not vendors'**. Sovereignty-by-name. Strongest archetype-direct match — brand-book §4 says sovereignty is *the* defining quality, and "keep" is the verb of sovereignty.

Counter: Google Keep is the elephant collision. While different category (notes app), the cognitive priming is real. New users hearing "Rodkeep" may parse as "another Keep app" — the contrast Rodix-vs-ChatGPT is harder to make if the user mental-models Rodkeep as Keep-adjacent.
