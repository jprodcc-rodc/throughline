## Etymology and meaning

Coined compound: **Rod** (founder pseudonym Rodc) + **span** (EN word: bridge across, extent of time/space, distance between two points). Direct semantic carry of *"continuity-of-thought across time"* — the friends-intro line 311 metaphor "the spine of conversations, which is what cards give you" expressed as one word.

**Etymology of span:** Old English *spann* (a hand's-breadth, by extension distance between two points), proto-Germanic *spannan* (to stretch, draw tight). Carries both extent (across time) and tension (held between).

## Strategic positioning

Rodspan positions as **the memory layer that spans across AI conversations / models / time**. The name itself carries the brand-bet (continuity-of-thought) instead of the brand teaching the name. This is Rodspan's strongest argument vs Rodix.

Counter-argument: Naming the brand after one bet (continuity) locks the brand to that bet. If Rodix evolves to be more about cross-model than continuity, "Rodspan" reads off. Empty-coined names have optionality — Stripe pivoted from card-stripe to embedded finance without renaming.
